Q: What is NEFT?
A: NEFT is a nationwide electronic funds transfer system that settles payments in half hourly batches, available 247.
Q: What is RTGS?
A: RTGS is a real time gross settlement system used for high value transfers with immediate settlement, available 247.
Q: What is IMPS?
A: IMPS is an instant interbank electronic fund transfer service available 247 across banks and channels.
Q: What is UPI?
A: UPI is an instant payment system enabling transfers using UPI ID, mobile number, QR code, or account+IFSC, 247.
Q: Is NEFT available 247?
A: Yes, NEFT operates 247 in half hourly settlement cycles.
Q: Is RTGS available 247?
A: Yes, RTGS runs round the clock with real time settlement.
Q: Are IMPS transfers instant?

Q: Are UPI transfers instant?
A: Yes, UPI payments are typically instant across participating banks.
